Summary

Dublin, the capital of Ireland, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, friendly locals, and lively culture. Situated on the east coast, Dublin offers a mix of historic landmarks, modern attractions, and a bustling social scene. Visitors are drawn to its charming streets, iconic pubs, and cultural institutions.

Beer Culture

Dublin's beer culture is legendary, with the city being home to the world-famous Guinness brewery. Visitors can explore traditional pubs, craft beer bars, and brewery tours. Key places to visit include the Guinness Storehouse, The Bernard Shaw, and The Glimmer Man.

Hurling

Hurling is a traditional Irish sport with a rich history. During our trip, we will attend a hurling game at Parnell Park on Saturday at 15:30. This is a fantastic opportunity to experience one of Ireland's most exciting sports.

Weather

During our trip from 13.9.24 to 16.9.24, we can expect mild temperatures ranging from 10°C to 18°C. The weather is likely to be partly cloudy with occasional showers, so packing a light raincoat is advisable.

Necessary Documents

Travelers from Switzerland to Dublin need a valid passport or national ID card. Ensure your documents are up to date before the trip.

Flight Details

We will fly with Swiss airline. Flight LX400 departs at 10:50 from Zürich (ZHR) to Dublin, and flight LX401 departs at 13:00 from Dublin back to Zürich. We will meet on the SBB train from Bern to Zürich Flughafen, departing Bern at 8:02.

Accommodation

Money
